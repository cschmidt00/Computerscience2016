Hardware!

Hardware is all of the things inside of your computer that make it able to do all of
the amazing things that it does. There area There are 5 essentials when it comes to
hardware, there are motherboards, graphics cards, CPUs, RAM, and harddrives.
(Hover over each for more information about each part)

Motherboard

The motherboard is a circut that connects all of the other parts in
the computer letting them communicate with eachother.

CPU

The CPU is the central processing unit and what it does is it tells all of the other parts what to do
and basically gives instructions to other parts on what to do.

Graphics Card

The Graphics card does video calculations to make the screen of your computer
to display what you want it to display and this makes the CPUs work easier

RAM

RAM stands for Random Access Memory, this is where information
that is only needed for a short time and dont need to be saved go.

Harddrive

Harddrives are your main, perminate storage on your computer. This is where
files that you download are saved and the computer does not delete these files unless you tell it to.
